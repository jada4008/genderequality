Famous Feminists



What is a feminist?

Feminists are people who support the advocacy of womens rights on the basis of equality of the sexes. There are many women who were able to help in this very long fight against inequality. Some of these women are:



Betty Friedan

Writer, activist, and feminist

Gloria Steinem

Social political activist, journalist, and feminist

Coretta Scott King

Author, Civil rights leader, activist, and feminist

Maya Angelou

Poet, singer, memoirist, Civil Rights leader, activist, and feminist

Oprah Winfrey

Media proprietor, talk show hostess, actress, producer, philanthropist, and feminist
